# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-10

Expense Guard ("the App") is an offline-first app. Your data stays on your device.

## Data We Collect
- **We do not collect or transmit personal data to our servers.**
- All expense, subscription, and settings data is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage (IndexedDB).

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

## Ads
- The app may display ads for free users.
- If you integrate an ad network, that network may collect data as described in their policies.

## Permissions
- The app does not require special device permissions beyond normal web access.

## Children’s Privacy
- The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Your Choices
- You can delete your data at any time by clearing your browser/site data.
- You can export and import your data using the in‑app backup feature.

## Changes to This Policy
